Searchblock - Bookmarklet Based History Remover

How To Get Searchblock History Remover

Copy The Code Below:

javascript:location.replace('https://google.com');

How To Add The Bookmarklet

1. Right/two-finger click your bookmarks bar

2. Click "Bookmark Manager"

3. In the bookmark manager click the ⋮ icon in the top right of your screen

4. Click "Add new bookmark"

5. Enter whatever name you want (you don't have to have one)

6. Paste the code that you copied earlier into the box labled URL

6. Click "Save"

How To Use

When you are done with on a page that you do not want on your browser history all you have to do is click the bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar that you made and you will be redirected to google.com because it is not suspicous and if you redirect to about:blank your page stays on your history, after that, your original page will not show up on your browser history.

Keep In Mind That...

1. Searchblock History Remover is only really needed if admin settings prevent you from deleting your history.

2. Searchblock History Remover does not clear past history, it prevents a page from going to you browser history.

3. Searchblock History Remover should not be overused to the point where your browser history is practicaly empty, if you ask me, thats pretty suspicous.
