Fun Bookmarklets To Mess Around With

A bookmarklet is a bookmark in your bookmarks bar that instead of bringing you to a website, runs a line of JavaScript in your current tab so that you can do things like change news headings to prank your friends without inspect element, just like in MS Word or Google Documents. Below here are some bookmarklets to add to your bookmarks bar.
